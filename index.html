Julien Chris

Julien Chris is a French DJ making his move into House and Deep House after years as a generalist. Passionate about these sounds, he has been refining his style to create a unique musical experience.

In Paris, he has built a reputation for performing at exclusive private events for elite audiences, delivering sophisticated and immersive sets. As a manager of rising talents like Papa Drag, he also plays a key role in shaping the next generation of artists.

Now, he is preparing a major project in Berlin, marking his definitive entry into the House and Deep House scene.
